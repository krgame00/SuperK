# Product

## Register
product

## Users
Manga readers, scanlation groups, and enthusiasts who want to translate raw manga (e.g., Japanese, Korean, Chinese) into Thai quickly and seamlessly.

## Product Purpose
SuperK Manga Translator is a web-based tool that uses AI and computer vision (OpenCV) to detect speech bubbles, cleanly erase original text using smart masking, and overlay translated text in real-time. It aims to reduce the manual labor of manga translation.

## Brand Personality
Sleek, Elegant, Minimalist. The design should get out of the way of the content (the manga pages) while providing a premium, professional feel.

## References
- https://toriitranslate.com/image (Clean workspace, focused canvas, minimal distraction).

## Anti-references
- Cluttered SaaS dashboards.
- Overly playful or stereotypically "anime" styles (no bright neon colors, no chibi mascots).
- Default AI aesthetics (no generic gray/cream backgrounds with wide drop shadows).

## Design Principles
1. **Content is King**: The manga page is the hero. The UI should fade into the background.
2. **Focus**: Only show tools when they are needed.
3. **Elegance through Restraint**: Use typography and subtle spacing rather than heavy borders or cards to separate elements.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast (≥4.5:1) for all text against backgrounds.
- Clear, readable typography for the translated text overlay.
